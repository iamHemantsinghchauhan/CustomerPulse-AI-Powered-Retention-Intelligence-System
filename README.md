## 🌟 Project Overview

CustomerPulse is an end-to-end Machine Learning solution designed to help businesses proactively identify customers at risk of churn and implement data-driven retention strategies.

The system combines customer analytics, predictive modeling, risk assessment, interactive visualizations, and automated reporting into a unified retention intelligence platform.

By transforming customer behavior data into actionable business insights, organizations can improve customer loyalty, reduce churn, and protect long-term revenue.

---

## 🎯 Business Problem

Customer acquisition is expensive.

Losing existing customers directly impacts revenue, profitability, and long-term business growth.

This project addresses the challenge by:

- Predicting customer churn probability
- Identifying high-risk customers
- Analyzing churn-driving factors
- Generating retention recommendations
- Delivering executive-ready business reports

Customers with higher balances demonstrated increased churn risk.

### 🔴 Older Customers
Older customer segments showed elevated churn probability.

### 🔴 Inactive Members
Inactive customers were significantly more likely to leave.

### 🔴 Credit Score Impact
Lower credit score segments displayed moderate churn tendencies.
